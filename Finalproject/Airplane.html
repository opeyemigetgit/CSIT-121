The History of Airplane

The invention of the airplane revolutionized transportation and paved the way for modern aviation. Here's a brief history:

Early Years

The first successful powered flight was achieved by the Wright brothers on December 17, 1903, in Kitty Hawk, North Carolina. Their Wright Flyer aircraft flew for 12 seconds and covered a distance of 120 feet.

VIDEO OF THE FIRST AIRPLANE FLIGHT.

Commercial Aviation

In the 1920s, the first commercial airlines began offering passenger flights. These early planes were often slow and uncomfortable, but paved the way for modern air travel.
