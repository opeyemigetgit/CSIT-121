Cooking

As a person who likes to eat, I am equally one that likes to cook. I am what one would call a “foodie” since I like to try all different kinds of food. Same as my diverse music taste I have a very well-rounded appreciation of food from all over the world. My favorite foods if I had to choose would be street tacos, kimchi stew, and anything chocolate!

My favorite thing to cook would be anything revolving around breakfast or brunch food: shrimp and grits, breakfast sandwiches/tacos, pancakes, french toast, etc. I love to experiment with different flavors and ingredients when I can. My housemate and I make an effort to go to the library to check out different cookbooks that interest us.

In a perfect world, I would drop everything to go to culinary school and become a personal chef, just so I can cook on a smaller scale while also being able to share my food.
