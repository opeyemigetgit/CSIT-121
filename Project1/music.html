Music

One thing that I try to do any chance I have is listen to music! I am a full believer in “music is therapy”, so chances are if you see me I am probably walking around with one earbud in. Music is not just an outlet but is my way of coping with the busy world around me.

I have a very broad taste in music; what I listen to is determined by the kind of mood I’m in. I listen to almost anything you can think of, from rap to country, from R&B to rock, and even listen to music from other places in the world like Korea and Cuba.

One thing most people don’t know about me is that I have an extreme love and appreciation of jazz music. This dates back to my early childhood when my late uncle Prince and I would sit on the front porch listening to Ray Charles. Then my 8th-grade science teacher introduced me to Gregory Porter and I instantly was hooked.

Since I am a really big Disney fan, I also like to just listen to soundtracks from my favorite Disney movies. When I study, I typically go on Spotify and listen to whatever Lofi playlist I can find. I would love to learn to play an instrument myself one day, but until then I will continue to appreciate the art of music from others.
